ABOUT BOLIVIA RAFTING

RAFTING:

Rafting is an exciting activity that allows you to descend mighty rivers in inflatable rafts, facing rapids of varying difficulty. In Bolivia, it's a unique experience thanks to its pristine natural landscapes, from the lush Amazon rainforest to the inter-Andean valleys. Ideal for adventurers seeking adrenaline and connection with nature. Our mision is promote community development in the municipality and environmental awareness. We offer adventure tours in Villa Tunari, the tourist capital of Cochabamba. Bolivia Rafting works with experts

HISTORY

The history of rafting began a few years ago, around 2021. Previously, the community dedicated itself to tourism, offering hikes within the reserve, and selling handicrafts to visitors.

Years later, they saw rafting as an opportunity to draw attention to the beautiful rivers in the area. Some comunity members got IRF-certificates in order to offer safe and exciting experiences.
