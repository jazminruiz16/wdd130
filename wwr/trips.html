River Rafting Trips

What Makes All-Outdoors Different?

Transportation to and from Villa Tunari, safety briefing, and instructions at the start of the trip. No swimming skills are required; our guides are certified by the International Rafting Federation.

Contact Us

About the Trip

We start with a group of at least 5 people. All members receive a safety briefing.

Leap of confidence into the water, from a 6-meter springboard-type platform

A safety and command briefing will be held at the start, followed by rafting on approximately 12 km of river.

Route Options

Beginners route

The route will take place in calm waters with gentle currents, without losing the adrenaline rush of rafting through one of the national parks and its diverse flora and fauna. Ideal for beginners and leisurely excursions.

Advanced route

The route will take place on challenging rapids with significant waves. Advanced skills and experience are required. An exciting and challenging activity for those seeking thrills in spectacular natural settings
